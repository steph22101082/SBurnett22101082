A Trait That Had an Impact on My Academic Life


A trait/emotion that impacted me throughout my school life is discipline. Although not necessarily considered an emotion, it's a good characteristic to keep if you want to succeed in life. Many times I've felt dejected and tired and want to give up throughout my years of attending school, but the discipline of wanting to achieve something great keeps me from staying down. Even if I'm at my lowest, I try to at least take a break and then get right back to what needs to be done, whether I want to or not. At the end of the day, it's up to me to be successful or not.

My Hero

Mike Tyson

Mike Tyson is a legendary former professional boxer renowned for his exceptional talent and remarkable career. What makes Tyson an inspiring hero to many is his unwavering discipline and commitment to his craft. Despite facing numerous challenges and setbacks, Tyson's relentless work ethic and dedication to boxing propelled him to become the youngest heavyweight champion in history. His ability to overcome adversity and maintain his focus in the face of adversity serves as a compelling example of what discipline can achieve. Mike Tyson's story inspires me because it underscores the incredible feats one can accomplish through sheer determination and hard work.


Mike Tyson once said, "Discipline is doing what you hate to do but doing it like you love it." I find this to be very true because that's what's needed to get through the hardest times one can.
